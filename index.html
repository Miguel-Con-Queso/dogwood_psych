About

Dogwood Psychological Services is located in Johnson City, TN and specializes in psychological assessments. It is owned and operated by Dr. Bess Lewis, a licensed clinical psychologist with extensive experience in assessing and diagnosing both children and adults. Originally from Chattanooga, Dr. Lewis is passionate about serving East Tennessee.

Services

Comprehensive Child Assessments assess for mood disorders, ADHD, Autism Spectrum Disorder, learning disorders, as well as behavioral concerns in children and adolescents under age 18.

Adult Assessments are more focused assessments that assess for ADHD, ASD, and mood/personality disorders in adults.

Individual Therapy is available on a limited basis for physicians, therapists, ministers, educators, nurses, and other helping professions in Appalachia.

What to Expect in a Psych Assessment

Interview
60-90 minutes

Dr. Lewis meets with you to gain important background information about what you are experiencing and your sense of what may be going on. Children participate in this interview, too.

Testing
2-4 Hours, 1-2 Sessions

Dr. Lewis administers a number of standardized tests designed to answer your questions. Cognitive (IQ), academic, neuropsychological, and/or personality tests may be given.

Report + Feedback Session
60-90 minutes

The results of the assessment are written in a comprehensive report with recommendations for your home life, school/work, and further treatment. Dr. Lewis meets with you to clearly explain the important pieces of the report.
